Frequently asked questions

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes — cancel anytime from your account settings. You'll retain access until the end of your current billing period with no charges after that.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), and popular wallets. All payments are secured with 256-bit encryption.
Is there a student discount?
Yes! Students with a valid .edu email get 40% off the Pro plan. Enter your institutional email at checkout and we'll verify it automatically.
What happens to my PDFs if I downgrade?
Your uploaded documents are retained for 30 days after downgrading. You can re-upload after upgrading again. We'll send you a reminder email before any data is removed.
Do you offer refunds?
We offer a 7-day full refund guarantee — no questions asked. Simply email us within 7 days of your purchase and we'll process the refund within 3–5 business days.
